About Us

The Center for Internet of Things (C-IoT) aims to promote the Internet of Things (IoT) based Ecosystem that will open numerous avenues in terms of Knowledge Enhancement, Employability, Entrepreneurship, Institute-Industry Collaboration, Indigenous Product Development and Global Recognition. The building infrastructure facilities for the Center has been created under 110- announcement (2015- 2016) of Tamil Nadu Government for the cost of 5 crores. The foundation stone was laid by former Honorable Vice Chancellor Prof. M.K.Surappa on 10th June 2018 and subsequently inaugurated on 4th September 2019. From 28th February 2022, the Centre has been approved with full fledged autonomous status vide syndicate of Anna University. The functioning of the Centre commence from 27th of April 2022.

Vision

The C-IoT perseveres in becoming a Centre of Excellence in IoT for carrying out cutting edge interdisciplinary research in IoT across all disciplines, offering consultancy and imparting high quality training of international reputation to the students, researchers, faculties and industrial professionals. It strives to become a preferred partner by the industry and community for providing Engineering solutions in the area of IoT.

Mission

The C-IoT shall contribute to the educational, economic and social development by carrying out R & D Projects in the cutting- edge area in IoT involving interdisciplinary teams and come up with patentable ideas and renowned publications. Engage in Consultancy work in the area of IoT to cater to the needs of the industries. Provide a strong foundation in IoT through the training programs to the students, researchers, faculties and industrial professionals. Promote and guide aspiring Entrepreneurs in the area of IoT for their establishment. Transfer IoT Technology and its application to Government Departments and various users through Pilot Projects/ Operational Projects. Provide academic support to undergraduate / postgraduate / Ph.D / M.S/ PDF programmes of the University in the area of IoT.
